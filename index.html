A Short Background



Legend has it that Robert Johnson sold his soul to the Devil in oder to become a legend of Blues. Robert Johnson's life was not an easy one.

Johnson's fisrt wife died when giving birth to their first child.
Her parents, filed with grief, blamed Johnson for her death claiming it is because
he played the devil's music.
Later on in his life Johnson had another child with a woman,
this time however, her parents refused for him to see the child or his mother.
Yet again his passion for the blues was seen as an act of siding with the devil.
